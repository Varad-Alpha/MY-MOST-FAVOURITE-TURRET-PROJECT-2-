This is my most favourite project, for so many reasons, one of them being, this is the first project i made with a team (online ofc), I guided with the code and hardware instructions, and also that this beautifully worked, and that means shooting everyone, but who minds, anyways, i hope you have a blast (like the turret) reading this

HOW IT WORKS(yay): The very precise turret constantly captures frames from the state of the artttt ESP32-CAM and processes them with a not so simple face detection algorithm. When it detects a face, the system calculates the error between the face’s position and the center of the frame. It then adjusts the pan and tilt servos(the hardest part) to keep the face centered. Once the target is centered and the cooldown period is over, the firing mechanism engages automatically.(woosh) It activates motors(basically like motorised nerf guns) to “shoot” at the target. This loop repeats in real time, enabling the turret to track and respond to moving targets on its own.
